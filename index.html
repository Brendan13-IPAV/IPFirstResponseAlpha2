Welcome to the IP Options Finder

This tool can help you identify potential pathways for protecting or enforcing your intellectual property rights in Australia. Based on your selections, you will be provided with a list of options that may be worth considering given your circumstances.

Intellectual property (IP) rights are valuable business assets that can provide competitive advantages. Understanding how to effectively protect and enforce these rights is essential for businesses and creators. While there are many pathways available, finding the right approach for your specific situation can sometimes be challenging.

This tool aims to simplify the process by helping you navigate through various IP protection and enforcement options based on your specific needs and circumstances. From patents and trade marks to design rights and copyright, our goal is to provide you with information about potential strategies that align with your situation.

Please note that the information provided through this tool is general in nature and does not constitute legal advice. For specific guidance tailored to your individual circumstances, you might want to consult with an IP professional.
